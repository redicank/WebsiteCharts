Scores represent combined overall evaluation ratings on a 1–5 scale. Unlike the University of Richmond data, MSU departmental averages were not reported per question but as a single overall figure. Semesters marked COVID coincide with remote or hybrid instruction during the pandemic.

* The original MSU evaluation scale runs in the opposite direction (1 = best, 5 = worst). Scores here have been inverted (displayed as 6 − original score) so that higher values consistently indicate stronger performance, matching the intuitive presentation used throughout this page.

* My individual scores are included within the departmental averages, as the data are not provided in a disaggregated form that would allow for their removal.
